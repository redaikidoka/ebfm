How many fish are in the water?

NOAA research ships and chartered fishing vessels conduct annual surveys of the Bering Sea and Gulf of Alaska, and biennial surveys in the Aleutians. This allows managers to adjust catch limits whenever necessary to keep fishing levels sustainable.

Pollock: rebuilt after foreign overfishing

Confronting unsustainable pollock fishing by foreign fleets, in the late 1970s the newly formed North Pacific Fishery Management Council immediately slashed harvests on pollock in the Eastern Bering Sea. The Council now strives to keep fishing pressure in the “safe zone”.
